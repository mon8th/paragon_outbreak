- Paragon University students and professors (in-game reference)
- Players who enjoy classic shooter games
- Fans of retro FPS and survival horror

## Gameplay Loop

**Start**
Players spawn inside the campus (5th floor) and go through a tutorial on how to move, shoot, and pick up items.

**Mid Game**
Navigate through each designated room to find resources and keys. Zombie encounters increase in frequency and difficulty as you progress downward.

**End Game**
Reach the escape point in the lower level lobby, survive the final zombie encounter, and view your statistics.

## Weapon Progression

| Floor | Weapons Available |
|-------|-------------------|
| Floor 5 | Box cutter, metal ruler, fire axe |
| Floor 4 | Baseball bat, golf club, improvised weapons |
| Floor 3 | Taser (from dead security guard) |
| Floor 2 | Pistol (Dean's office safe - code: 1995) |
| Floor 1 | Second pistol (dead guard), Shotgun (security office) |
| Lower Level | Melee upgrades (kitchen knives, crowbar) |

**Total Ammo Available:** Approximately 60 pistol rounds, 20 shotgun shells
